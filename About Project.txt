This project is basically a real time news app which allows the users to create local content themselves
Users will be able to login using their fb accounts or email addresses.
Stories or Posts will be made using either text or text and a photo to create a kind of news article.
Every story will have a tag, this tag can be location or just descriptive.
Users will be able to select what they want to see in their newsfeeds by selecting their tags in settings.
newsfeed will be continous feed of stories from the tag with the latest first.
Fame is the the amount of likes, shares or dislikes a story from a user gets.
The user will be able to see a list of their stories and their fam in their profile page.

The aim of this app is to get people creating news stories for their own areas or cities and to get the people around them engaged.
